Who is Black Eyed Peas?

Black Eyed Peas is an American musical group consisting of rappers will.i.am, apl.de.ap, Taboo and J.Rey Soul. They were Originaly an alternative hip hop group.

Original Members

Fergie(2002 -2016)

Fergie Duhamel, professionally known by her stage name Fergie, is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. She first achieved chart success as part of the hip hop group the Black Eyed Peas.

will.i.am(Since 1995)

William Adams (born March 15, 1975), better known by his stage name will.i.am (pronounced "Will-I-am"), is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, entrepreneur, and actor. He is the founding and lead member of the musical group Black Eyed Peas.
